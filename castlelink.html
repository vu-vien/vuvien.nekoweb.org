Spreading castle, on the web!

What

It's about linking castle.xyz by using an 88x31 badge/button inserted on websites, a simple way to spread out castle on the web.

Why

It's cool

Castle is cool, obviously!

More attention

This might help castle getting more attention.

Rules

• Make sure that the castle linking badge/button is clickable, and is linked to castle.xyz website.
• Place the badge/button somewhere that's eye-catching, and not being covered under something else.
• The badge/button should be inserted on personal websites (it's made by you!).

How

Markdown

Copy the texts on the box below, then paste it into your markdown file (.md) or rentry editor (rentry.co).

[![Castle badge](insert image link here)](https://castle.xyz)

Tutorial video


HTML

Copy the texts on the box below, then paste it into your html file.

<a href="https://castle.xyz"><img src="insert badge link here"></a>
